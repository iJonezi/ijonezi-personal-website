    • Meet Carson!

    Post Date: 4/8/2024

    Hello! Welcome to my page. Here, I showcase my achievements as a Software Engineer, the things I have learned from my individual projects, as well as some career information about me. Growing up, I was fascinated by technology and desired to learn more. I decided to venture into programming when I was 14 years old playing a game called Garry's Mod. There, I learned my first programming language, Lua. Since then, I was hooked on development in it's entirety and eventually tried different languages.

    I have been a developer for several gaming communities to include communities on Garry's Mod, FiveM/RedM, Arma 3, to where I am now, outside from games, working with the State of Alaska. In my day to day, I develope, test, and maintain network systems, computing systems, and programs written by me.

    Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or send me an email if you'd like to learn more, or are interested in having me work with you!
